CONTEST DETAILS

The Young Fashion Designers Contest aims to encourage fashion enthusiasts to showcase their talent by modifying existing shirts into innovative and appealing designs. Whether you use your own shirt or the mystery shirt we provide, the possibilities are endless!

HOW TO ENTER

OPEN TO ALL YOUNG FASION ENTHUSIASTS.

Submission Process

Send the modified shirt and write-up to our mailing address. Digital submissions of the shirt photos and write-up are also accepted.

Judging Criteria

Creativity, originality, craftsmanship, and overall aesthetic appeal.
